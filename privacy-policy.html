Privacy Policy for Blackjack Helper

Last updated: October 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Blackjack Helper is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • App Usage Data: Card counting statistics, betting records, and game preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • No Network Data: The app works entirely offline and does not transmit data to external servers

2. How We Use Information

All data collected by Blackjack Helper is used solely for:

  • Providing card counting functionality
  • Displaying betting recommendations
  • Storing your game history locally
  • Improving app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage:

  • Local Storage: All game data, settings, and history are stored on your device only
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync or backup your data to any external servers
  • Data Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time

4. Third-Party Services

Blackjack Helper does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your locally stored data:

  • Data is encrypted using device-level security
  • No data transmission occurs over the internet
  • App permissions are minimal and necessary only for core functionality

6. Children's Privacy

Blackjack Helper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app or using the app's reset function
  • Control what data is stored through app settings
